## 🐧 About the Project

The **TVBox Project – IFSP Salto** is a community outreach initiative from the [Federal Institute of São Paulo, Salto Campus](https://slt.ifsp.edu.br/), focused on repurposing **TVBoxes seized by the Brazilian Federal Revenue Service** into tools for digital inclusion.

The process involves the complete **decharacterization** of the devices — removing the proprietary OS and installing Linux distributions tailored for ARM architecture — followed by their **donation to underprivileged public schools**, expanding access to technology for communities that need it most.

Beyond the repurposing process, the project retains a portion of the devices for internal use in **research, development, and experimentation**, producing open-source tools, custom operating system images, and technical documentation for the broader community.
